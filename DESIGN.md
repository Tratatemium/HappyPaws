## Project Background
HappyPaws is an animal rescue organization seeking a mobile-first landing page to showcase pets available for adoption. The design must encourage exploration, highlight urgent cases, and allow users to favorite pets.

---

## Goals
- **Mobile-first experience:** Prioritize small screens and vertical scrolling.
- **Browse pets:** Easy, fast scrolling through available animals.
- **Favorites:** Users can add pets to a favorites list for follow-up.
- **Urgent adoption:** Visually highlight pets needing urgent adoption or with special stories.
- **Playful, intuitive, responsive:** Fun layout, simple interactions, touch-friendly.

---

## Target User
- Pet lovers browsing on mobile.
- Interested in adopting or following pets.
- Not necessarily tech-savvy; interface must be easy and enjoyable.

---

## Device Considerations
- **Primary:** Mobile (touch screens, vertical scroll).
- **Secondary:** Tablet/Desktop.
- **Touch-friendly:** Large buttons, cards, easy interactions.
- **Performance:** Fast scrolling, quick discovery.

---

## Pages & User Flow

### 1. Landing Page
- **Header:** Logo, playful tagline, hamburger menu.
- **Featured pets carousel:** Highlight urgent cases.
- **Navigation at the footer:** Galery, chats, user page.

### 2. Gallery
- **Pet cards:** Grid of animals with photo, name, age.
- **Urgent badge:** Visual marker for urgent adoption.
- **Add to Favorites:** Paw shaped button with feedback (animation/change color).
- **Infinite scroll:** Load more pets as user scrolls.

### 3. Card / Animal Profile
- **Large photo, name, age, breed, story.**
- **Description story:** illnesses, personality, history, etc..
- **Urgent highlight if applicable.**
- **Add to Favorites button.**
- **Adopt button:** Leads to checkout.
- **Possible:** Carousel gallery with more pictures.

### 4. Adoption Checkout
- **Info from shelter:**
- **Simple form:** User details.
- **Terms of adoption and contract.** (collapsable)
- **Confirmation button.**

### 5. Chat (Popup/Page)
- **Contact rescue:** Quick questions about pets.
- **Mobile-friendly chat UI.**

### 6. Favorites Page
- **List of favorited pets.**
- **Remove from favorites.**
- **Quick access to adopt or view profile.**

---

## Wireframes (Suggested Structure)
- **Landing:** Logo, featured pets carousel, CTA, nav footer.
- **Gallery:** Scrollable cards, urgent badges, favorite buttons.
- **Profile:** Large image, details, urgent highlight, actions.
- **Checkout:** Ifo, form, confirmation.
- **Chat:** Simple, mobile chat bubble.
- **Favorites:** List/grid of favorited pets.

---

## Visual Design
- **Playful colors:** Soft backgrounds, accent colors for urgent pets.
- **Rounded cards/buttons:** Friendly, approachable.
- **Urgent pets:** Use badges, colored borders, or subtle animation.
- **Feedback:** Heart button animates when favorited.
- **Typography:** Large, readable fonts.

---

## Interactions
- **Add to Favorites:** Tap heart, visual feedback (fill/animate).
- **Urgent pets:** Badge or highlight, not overwhelming.
- **Scroll:** Smooth, fast, infinite loading.
- **Responsive:** Adapts to tablet/desktop.

---

## Accessibility
- **Contrast:** Sufficient for readability.
- **Touch targets:** Large enough for fingers.
- **Alt text:** All images.
- **Keyboard navigation:** For desktop.

---

## Bonus
- **Favorites page:** Browse favorited pets, quick actions.

---

## Deliverables
- Wireframes (low/high fidelity).
- Mobile-first HTML/CSS prototype:
  - Pet listing section.
  - Highlighted urgent pets.
  - Add to Favorites buttons with feedback.
  - Bonus: Favorites page.
